## Tips for faster approval

1. **Complete your site first** — reviewers check murallwallpaper.com. Make sure /partners and /journal pages are live.
2. **Be specific about audience** — "interior designers and homeowners" converts better than "home decor fans"
3. **Mention trade programme** — 2,400 trade members signals B2B credibility
4. **Apply to networks first** — ShareASale and Awin approve publishers within 1–3 days, then you apply to individual brands inside
5. **Follow up after 5 days** if no response — a short email doubles approval rates
